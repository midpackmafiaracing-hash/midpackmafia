Series We Race In

Mid Pack Mafia races alongside a handful of communities that give our drivers places to compete, improve, and stay plugged into the wider sim racing scene. These are the leagues and groups currently in our orbit.

TrakFiends X
Mid Pack Mafia

TrakFiends X Mid Pack Mafia

iRacing Series
League #: 14276
Focus: Collaborative iRacing competition between both communities.

This shared series represents one of the clearest partnerships on the schedule. It gives MPM drivers a place to race hard, stay connected with TrakFiends, and sharpen racecraft in organized iRacing competition.

NRUS

Noobs "R" Us

ACC & LMU Series
Platform: SimGrid-hosted events and community racing.
Focus: Assetto Corsa Competizione and Le Mans Ultimate competition.

NRUS gives the team another strong home for organized racing outside iRacing. It is a valuable part of the MPM mix for drivers who want structured ACC and LMU events while staying active with another established community.

NRUS on SimGrid NRUS Discord

TrakFiends

Beyond the joint MPM series, TrakFiends is an important standalone community in the rotation. It offers another competitive outlet for drivers who want to stay busy across both ACC and iRacing events.
